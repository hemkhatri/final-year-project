Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

At Marketplace Central, we value your trust and are fully committed to protecting your personal data, identity assets, and financial routing infrastructures. This Privacy Policy details precisely how we collect, process, secure, and disclose information across our customer platforms and extended Merchant Registration networks.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you explicitly submit to us, which falls broadly into two functional buckets:

  • General Accounts: Basic customer identity maps including names, phone configurations, verification email strings, and localized shipping/billing designations.
  • Merchant Registrations (KYC): Government-issued documentation scans (Passports or National Identities subject to automated OCR analysis), regional regulatory tax signatures (PAN / VAT cards), official business certification licenses, and clear banking distribution data streams (Account/Routing arrays).

2. How Your Data Is Handled

We process gathered information primarily to manage active workflows, specifically:

  • Authenticating identity bounds to run predictive counter-fraud checks during merchant verification sequences.
  • Managing execution logs governing automated escrow split routing protocols across active fulfillment pipelines.
  • Improving internal platform safety tools and ensuring regulatory tax compliance within local operational guidelines.

3. Document Security Infrastructure

All high-sensitivity merchant verification components (including checking files, license duplicates, and legal IDs) are stored using secure cloud storage networks with heavy encryption standards. Access to this data is strictly regulated, meaning only authorized compliance layers can access it for audit and validation checks.

4. Third-Party Data Disclosures

We do not trade your information for secondary commercial tracking. Data loops are shared strictly under these production-level conditions:

  • With accredited financial institutions and automated secure clearing systems to execute payout splits smoothly.
  • With designated shipping or third-party courier companies to arrange warehouse pickups and deliveries.
  • When legally mandated by law enforcement bodies or regulatory authorities to handle explicit compliance audits or fraud resolutions.

5. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

You retain the full right to update your profile data or request account deletion logs at any time. However, please note that certain identity records and financial history items must be safely archived for specific legal timeframes to comply with standard regional financial laws.

Privacy & Compliance Inquiries

If you have questions regarding data encryption protocols, OCR processing safety, or wish to pull your identity application out of our active pipelines, please reach out directly to privacy@marketplacecentral.com.
